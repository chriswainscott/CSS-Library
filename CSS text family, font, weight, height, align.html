The < a class ="breed" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/widiBriard"> Briard, or berger de brie, is a largge breed of all dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed Histroy

The briard, which is believed to have originiated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extention. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering Charlemagne, Napoleon, thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owend briards
